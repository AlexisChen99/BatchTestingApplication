    How does the simulator work?

    Testing for Covid-19 is extremely limited in the United States. Because of this, we can utilize batch testing to maximize the the medical resources we have. For example, with each sample taken, we can get up to two tests out of it and we can pool around up to 10 tests together to save testing in the case of samples that have low probability of testing positive for Covid-19.

    click the SIMULATE BATCH button to show the medical record numbers of the patients we should batch together based on the lowest probability algorithm. Click on RUN FULL SIMULATION to run through the entire sample collection and view how many tests were saved with the lowest probability batch testing algoritm used.
